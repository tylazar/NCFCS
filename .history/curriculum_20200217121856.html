CURRICULUM

At New College, we believe in the value of interdisciplinary education and flexibility in curriculum to meet each student's individual needs.

Instead of majors and minors, we have Areas of Concentrations (AOCs) and Slashes. Students wishing to having two AOCs as their primary educational focus can get a Double AOC in which the requirements of both AOCs are fully met. This system allows our students to focus on topics they're passionate in while still exploring other areas and learning from different perspectives. Each AOC has required courses for graduation, but the time these are taken and supplementary classes are up to the student and their advisor.

New College prides itself on its motivated and independent students.

New College students must satisfy both the requirements of the Liberal Arts Curriculum (LAC), New College's general education program, and the specific requirements for their major (Area of Concentration, or AOC), Double AOC, or minor (slash). With so many opportunities each term, the pathways below are provided as examples of how a student could complete the requirements for graduation.

We've put some checkpoints in place so that you make the most of your time at New College. Each term, you'll meet with your faculty advisor to discuss courses, tutorials, internships, and other academic experience.
