COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer science is a new and rapidly growing multi- and interdisciplinary field that uses advanced computing and data analysis to understand and solve complex problems.

Work closely with faculty to design a personalized plan of study.

Faculty have experience in the classroom as well as the business world including experience at Akamai, BBN, and MIT. You will work one-on-one with faculty to create a custom curriculum plan to prepare you for graduate school admission and a career in the technology field.

Get personalized feedback and advice in each class.

New College supports students with a passion for learning and a desire to take responsibility for their own education. That's why our professors provide narrative evaluations rather than letter grades to assess your work each term. These discussion-based assessments let instructors have a one-on-one conversation with the student to identify their strengths and weaknesses and develop a plan for future learning.

Create custom tutorials in collaboration with faculty.

Work with faculty members to create your own course in a specific area of interest. Past tutorials have included hacking, competitive programming, cryptography, and thesis-based research, but are limited only by your imagination.

Collaborate with peers and faculty alike to learn a range of skills and demonstrate your knowledge through practical applications.
