DEPARTMENT FEATURES

VR Lab

New College's Virtual Reality (VR) Lab offers students the opportunity to develop and test VR applications with the latest gear.

Tutorials

As a New College student, you have the ability to work with faculty to design your own course and conduct hands-on research in a personalized area of interest.

Heiser Complex

The 34,000 square-foot Heiser Natural Sciences Complex includes teaching and research labs for chemistry, biochemistry, biology, bioinformatics, computational science, mathematics, and physics.

Work closely with faculty to design a personalized plan of study.

Faculty have experience in the classroom as well as the business world. You will work one-on-one with faculty to create a custom curriculum to prepare you for graduate school admission and a career in the technology field.

Get personalized feedback and advice in each class.

New College supports students with a passion for learning and a desire to take responsibility for their own education. That's why our professors provide narrative evaluations rather than letter grades to assess your work each term.
