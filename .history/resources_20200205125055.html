VR Lab

New College's Virtual Reality (VR) Lab offers students the opportunity to develop and test VR applications with the latest gear.

Tutorials

As a New College student, you have the ability to work with faculty to design your own course and conduct hands-on research in a personalized area of interest.

Heiser Complex

The 34,000 square-foot Heiser Natural Sciences Complex includes teaching and research labs for chemistry, biochemistry, biology, bioinformatics, computational science, mathematics, and physics.
